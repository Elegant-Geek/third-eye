MEET THE BAND

Serj Tankian:
Curious, introspective, and a little cautious. He prefers to experience things directly rather than observe and is always asking questions. He likes to unwind with tea and hot showers.

Daron Malakian:
Fiery, impulsive, and reactive. He wears his heart on his sleeve and is easily set off by upsetting or stressful events. He cares deeply for the wellbeing of Serj in particular.

Shavo Odadjian:
Caring, enthusiastic, and laidback. He prefers to avoid confrontation and provides some comic relief. He enjoys smoking weed and tends to go along with whatever's happening.

John Dolmayan:
The stubborn logical member of the group. He has no problem taking charge of situations or expressing his thoughts. He likes reading and napping despite his tough, hard-to-read exterior.
